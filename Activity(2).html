Thai Artist (นักแสดงชาวไทย)

"Ciize" Rutricha Phapakithi

"Ciize" Rutricha Phapakithi is an actor born in Bangkok, Thailand. In 2022, Ciize received a bachelor's degree in Chinese for International Trade from the Chinese International College at Bangkok University. Her nickname, Ciize, comes from the Chinese "Xi Shi" (张西施), one of the legendary Four Beauties. Her father chose it to influence her to grow up as beautiful as the Four Beauties. Her younger sister, "Cherry" Phitsinee Saejung (or Cheriie), is a singer under the label Labo.

Since entering the entertainment industry in 2015, Ciize has been a talent under GMMTV. She was previously the main dancer of the project girl group SIZZY, formed by GMMTV in 2019. In 2023, they signed with Riser Music but disbanded in October 2024. On July 6, 2023, Ciize changed her name to Rutricha Phapakithi due to name duplication issues.

"Kapook" Ploynira Hiruntaveesin

"Kapook" Ploynira Hiruntaveesin, nickname Kapook, is a Thai actress and model under GMMTV. She graduated from a demonstration school affiliated with Srinakharinwirot University. Kapook has a bachelor's degree in business administration from Bangkok University. She is currently in a relationship with Channel 3 actor Krating Khunnarong.

At the age of 14, Kapook began booking commercials. In 2016, she had a supporting role as Orn in Senior Secret Love: My Lil Boy. Since then, she has taken part in a pile of dramas.

"Emi" Chayada Sangsri

"Emi" Thasorn Klinnium is an actor and beauty queen under GMMTV (จีเอ็มเอ็มทีวี). After graduating from Nawamintharachinuthit Horwang Nonthaburi School, she went on to study at Silpakorn University's Faculty of Information and Communication Technology.

In 2021, Emi made her acting debut in the second season of "Girl From Nowhere" (เด็กใหม่).

"Bonnie" Pattraphus Borattasuwan

"Bonnie" Pattraphus Borattasuwan is a Thai actor under GMMTV (จีเอ็มเอ็มทีวี). She got her start in the industry as model before signing with GMMTV and booking her debut role in their 2024 series "High School Frenemy" (มิตรภาพ คราบศัตรู).

"Film" Rachanun Mahawan

Rachanun Mahawan, nicknamed Film, is an actor under GMMTV. In December 2022, she graduated from the Faculty of Architecture Midea Art Branch at King Mongkut’s University of Technology Thonburi.

Film got her start in the industry when she won the competition 'GO ON GIRL & GUY: Star Search' (2019) by Clean & Clear and GMMTV.

"Namtan" Tipnaree Weerawatnodom

Tipnaree Weerawatnodom, nicknamed Namtan, is a Thai actress managed by GMM TV. Raised in Ayutthaya province, she graduated from the Faculty of Fine Arts—majoring in acting and directing—from Srinakharinwirot University. In 2024, Namtan graduated with a Master Degree in Performing Arts in the same Faculty.

She is the younger sister of Thai professional footballer Narubadin Weerawatnodom (Ton).

From 2013 to 2015, she was a well-known host of the show "Strawberry Krubcake." For her role in "Who Are You" (2020) playing twin sisters, Tipnaree was nominated as "Best Actress in a Leading Role" in the 25th "Asian Television Awards" (2021).

Outside her acting career, Tipnaree has an online shop of fish chili paste called Nam-Prik-Ta-Lood (IG: aroy_talood).

"Pond" Naravit Lertratkosum

"Pond" Naravit Lertratkosum is an actor and member of the project group JASP.ER (แจสเปอร์; Previously Project JASP.ER) under GMMTV (จีเอ็มเอ็มทีวี) and Riser Music (ไรเซอร์มิวสิค). He is currently studying Biomedical Engineering in the Faculty of Engineering at King Mongkut's Institute of Technology Ladkrabang.

In 2020, Pond was a winner of the third Go On Girl & Guy Star Search by Clean & Clear after which he was signed under GMMTV. During his university life, Pond was crowned the hottest guy of the Faculty of Engineering. On December 19, 2024, he made his debut with Project JASP.ER with the release of their debut single "Sadistic" (แรงอีกนิด).

Pond is the founder of the clothing brand Apollo.

"Phuwin" Tangsakyuen

Phuwin Tangsakyuen, nicknamed Phuwin, is an actor and singer-songwriter signed with GMMTV (จีเอ็มเอ็มทีวี) and Riser Music (ไรเซอร์มิวสิค). Born in Bangkok, Thailand, he attended international schools until year eleven when he received a GED and enrolled at Chulalongkorn University. He has since graduated from the university where he studied Information and Communication Engineering in the international programme. Apart from his mother tongue, he's fluent in English, and is also able to speak some Mandarin.

Phuwin began acting on television at the age of eleven, getting his first speaking role in the series "Sunshine, My Friend" (อรุณสวัสดิ์). In 2015, he performed in "The Sound of Music" at Myangthai Rachadalai Theatre.

Phuwin got his start as a singer primarily singing OSTs for his works with GMMTV. In 2024, he signed with Riser Music and released his first single under the label—which he wrote—"To You" (วันเกิดเธอ) on September 19, 2024.

"Fourth" Nattawat Jirochtikul

"Fourth" Nattawat Jirochtikul is an actor and singer from Bangkok, Thailand. He was nicknamed Fourth as he was born on King Rama IV's 200th birthday. After getting his start in the company's "Thailand School Star 2019" contest, Fourth signed with GMMTV (จีเอ็มเอ็มทีวี). He graduated from Saint Gabriel's College by taking the grade twelve equivalency test and is currently a student at Chulalongkorn University's Faculty of Law.

In 2021, Fourth made his acting debut in the series "F4 Thailand: Boys Over Flowers" (หัวใจรักสี่ดวงดาว). In May 2024, for his work on "Moonlight Chicken" (พระจันทร์มันไก่), he won the Outstanding Rising Star award at the Komchadluek Awards.

His first single, released in December 2022, was a collab cover of "Yak Rong Dang Dang" (อยากร้องดังดัง) as part of the soundtrack of the series "My School President" (แฟนผมเป็นประธานนักเรียน), in which he played his debut lead role. His first original solo single came a couple months later with "Let Me Tell You" (พูดได้ไหม), another track for the series. After a number of releases with GMMTV Records, he became an artist under Riser Music (ไรเซอร์มิวสิค) in 2024. His first single under the label, "เทคะแนน (Candidate)" was released on February 20, 2024.

He's the owner of the clothing brand Nu Mo Ne (นิว โม เนะ).

"Gemini" Norawit Titicharoenrak

"Gemini" Norawit Thiticharoenrak is a Thai actor and singer nicknamed after his zodiac sign. In May 2022, Gemini finished his secondary education at Harrow International School. Norawit is a Bachelor of Arts and Science in Integrated Innovation (BAScii) student at Chulalongkorn University.

Gemini first entered the entertainment industry when he competed in the GMMTV's star search competition "Thailand School Star 2019." Since then, Gemini has been signed under GMMTV (จีเอ็มเอ็มทีวี). Before joining the entertainment industry, Gemini participated in singing workshops and has performed in events such as school fairs.

In the beginning of 2023, Gemini's first solo single "Hook" (เพลงรัก) was released as part of the soundtrack of the series "My School President" (แฟนผมเป็นประธานนักเรียน), in which he played his debut lead role. After a number of releases under GMMTV Records, he became an artist under Riser Music (ไรเซอร์มิวสิค) in early 2024. On March 7, 2024, he released his first single under the label "Too Close to Handle" (ใกล้เกิน).

Gemini is the owner of the clothing brand Divine.

"Nanon" Korapat Kirdpan

"Nanon" Korapat Kirdpan, born in Bangkok, is an actor and singer signed with GMMTV and Riser Music. After graduating from Amatyakul School, he went on to study acting and directing at Srinakharinwirot University's College of Social Communication Innovation in the same generation as singer Jackie Jackrin and fellow GMMTV artist Perth Tanapon. Nanon is the son of actor Kunakorn Kirdpan. Furthermore, he is related to Vietnamese model and actress Chanunphat Kamolkiriluck (Gigie), since Nanon has Vietnamese ancestry from his maternal grandmother.

Nanon began in the entertainment industry young, shooting ads from only three months old. He is the co-owner of the fashion brand Dec.Fex and has a YouTube channel, Triple N.

"Ohm" Pawat Chittsawangdee

"Ohm" Pawat Chittsawangdee is a Thai actor under GMMTV born in Bangkok. In December 2022, Ohm graduated from the cinema and digital media programme at Srinakharinwirot University's College of Social Communication Innovation majoring in acting and directing.

Ohm began working in the entertainment industry with the leading role of Frame in the 2016 series "Make It Right." It was with his titular role in the 2019 film "Dew," though, that Ohm gained wide recognition.

In recognition of his acting abilities, he won Best Dramatic Scene for the GMMTV series "He's Coming To Me" at the LINE TV Awards (2020). He won Best Supporting Actor for the film "Dew" at the prestigious 28th Bangkok Critics Assembly Awards (2020).

Ohm owns the clothing brands Meheart.bkk and NO EXCUSE.
